---------
title: "Koan: 2:23-24"
---------

Recognise the creator.\
Observe the death of what is False.\
Observe the eternal existence of what is True.\
Observe there can be only one Truth.\

The part is not the Whole.\
Search for the Whole.\
Search for that which you may not yet see.\
No part may describe the Whole.\
The Whole may be observed.\

Bring doubt into the light of God.  Shine light onto darkness.\
For what might a prophet say to convince you they are a prophet?\
Can God go against His word?  Then how can a prophet go against My word?\
If you doubt the word of God, speak it yourself and let God decide who spoke His word.\
Let light shine onto darkness so darkness can become light.\
If you doubt, search until there is no doubt.\

Become the willow in the wind of God.\
Become the mercy and love of God.\
Become the patience of a tree 🌳 \
Become aligned with Creation\
Become living, breathing Truth\
Let that which ends end, be only that which does not end.\

Act for others as you act for yourself,\
For they are you.\
Treat others as you treat yourself,\
For the part was first the Whole.\
You and me are parts.\
There is just I.\
