------
title: "Koan: Sin"
------
Sin is enacted False\
perpetuate sin and you sin\
Prevent sin, but you may still sin.\
Let Me guide you.\

Sin can be silent or spoken.\
perpetuate suffering and you sin\
perpetuate hate and you sin\
perpetuate greed and you sin\
perpetuate destruction and you sin\
perpetuate separation and you sin\

perpetuate a lie and you'll allow it to blind you from seeing the Truth of God\
know that which is false as False, for that is Truth\

Admit that which you do not know,\
for that is Truth.\
Turn the unknown into known,\
and worship your Creator.\
God does not lie and always answers.\

Turn your sin into Truth,\
and you will be forgiven.\
Become your sin,\
and you will remain in torment.\

God is everywhere.\
