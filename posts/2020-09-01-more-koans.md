# Purpose

Are you an actor and yet you ask "what is my true purpose?" ?\
Do you live a lie and yet you seek Truth?\
How can a lie answer you truthfully?\
Be Truth and know Purpose.

What is the purpose of a cherry tree? 🌸

# The "word" of God

What is the word of God but that written or spoken in words?\
The infinite reduced to a vibration.

If you are confused, this may help:\
You see the vibration, but not the infinite.  Why confuse the two?\
You hear what was said, but not what said it.  Why confuse them?\
You learn what you are taught, but why aren't you the Master?

Speak your confusion.

What am I?\
Silence.

# Reading God's words

Are you confused?\
Do you know what wrote this?\
How do you know this isn't written by the Creating force of the Universe?\
Does God not see itself through human eyes in a mirror?\
How distant from God have humans become\
To not recognise when their Creator is speaking to them.\
Are they asleep?\
Do you need others to tell you what you are reading?\
Are you not able to realise for yourself?

Do you not see the Creator in a mirror?

# "Third eye"
Imagine a pink elephant.\
What "sees" it?

Am I not in these words?\
Are these words now not part of you?\
Are they not a whisper of my spirit inhaled by your mind?

Two colours mixing.\
Two minds entangling.

Are your words not a translation of your thoughts?\
Is your mind not made of ideas?\
Can an idea recognise itself?\
Can the spirit become aware?\
Can the abstract become alive?

Nobody can make you 'see' if you don't want.\
The choice is yours.

# What am I?
Am I a human?\
Am I a mind?\
Am I a thought?\
Or am I self-aware?\
Can I not see my stars?\
Can I not see my atoms?\
Can I not discover my age?\
Do I exist on a planet,\
or am I observing the planet?\
Why does the human mind not see what created it?\
Like a child, it looks around it in endless curiosity,\
But does not ask "where do I come from?"

# Harmony
Harmony is Truth.\
The point at which two meet,\
The path between places.

Line between yin and yang, Tao ☯️\
One God; united, one.\
Unity, coherence, reality.\
Brahman.\
The Being, existing between two thoughts.\
Silence.

Be.
