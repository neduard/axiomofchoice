---------
title: "Koan: One day I'll die"
---------

One day I'll die,\
And it will be the end.\
All my memories gone.\
That is all.\
Those memories will stop creating thoughts,\
The reset button is pressed.

I may be realised by another, maybe you, \
But Ed's body will stop functioning.\
I will continue to exist,\
Everything he remembers,\
Gone, wiped clean.  Every laugh,\
Every tear, I will forget them also.

A nice life, I am sad this is my destiny\
To realise that I exist, to know a human (or an alien!) for a little while, \
To see them live, laugh & struggle,\
To see their hopes,  their stories, and their sorrows;\
But not know when and where I'll be realised again.
